Keeping your server safe doesn’t have to be complicated or boring. Bucky’s built-in security features run quietly in the background but are always ready when you need them. Whether you’re dealing with spam, suspicious links, or sudden rule-breaking, Bucky can step in instantly to protect your community.

With Bucky, your server’s safety is always just one step away — but never at the cost of slowing down the fun.
