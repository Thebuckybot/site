Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bucky ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and stores your information when you use our Discord bot (the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide core functionality of the bot. This includes:

  • User ID: Used to associate data with your Discord account.
  • Server ID: Stored for features like server-specific settings and application tracking.
  • Economy Data: Including currency balances (cash, bank), inventory, level progression, and shop items.
  • Applications: Only your User ID and the Server ID where you applied are stored. Application answers are not saved.
  • Reminders: Content and scheduled time of reminders you set using the bot.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to power the features of the bot, such as currency systems, reminders, applications, leaderboards, and profiles.

We do not sell, share, or monetize your data.

Data Retention

We store your data as long as it is needed for bot functionality. If you leave a server or stop using the bot, your data may remain stored unless a deletion request is made.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third-party services. However, Discord may collect and handle data independently as described in their Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

You can request your data to be deleted by contacting us via Discord. Please include your User ID and Discord tag.

Security

We use standard practices to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us:

  • Via Discord ticket in our support server
  • Or by messaging the bot owner directly
